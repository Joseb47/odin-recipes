How to Make Bolognese Sauce

This homemade Bolognese sauce is dedicated to the late great Marcella Hazan

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • basil

Steps

  1. Boil water,cook pasta
  2. heat tomato sauce on medium
  3. drain water,mix tomato sauce and pasta
  4. final step add cheese and basil and enjoy!
