Carbonara

Spaghetti alla Carbonara

Carbonara is made with guanciale (cured pork), eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, spaghetti pasta, and lots of black pepper

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • basil

Steps

  1. Boil water,cook pasta
  2. heat tomato sauce on medium
  3. drain water,mix tomato sauce and pasta
  4. final step add cheese and basil and enjoy!
